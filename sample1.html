About Batman

Batman, also known as Bruce Wayne, is a fictional superhero appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics. He was created by artist Bob Kane and writer Bill Finger, and first appeared in Detective Comics #27 in 1939. Batman is a wealthy businessman who fights crime in Gotham City using his intellect, detective skills, and physical prowess. He is known for his iconic costume, which includes a cape and cowl, and his use of gadgets and technology to combat criminals.
